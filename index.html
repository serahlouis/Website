Hey there! I'm Serah Louis,


a Toronto-based journalist and author.



In my spare time, I read fantasy novels, bake pies, listen to the Bogfather (read: Hozier) and travel.

I hold an Honours Bachelor of Science from the University of Toronto where I completed a double major in Biology and Professional Writing and Communications – and wrote the draft of my very first published short story for a course on fantasy literature.

Surprisingly, most of my work today centres around personal finance and financial news in US and Canadian markets – but I like to pull inspiration from my background in life sciences when I can.
